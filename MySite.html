IDOLS FASHION

The number of products per group are generally almost the same. This website carries more apparel as compared to other goods such as fan lightsticks and so on. 

KPOPTOWN

Kpoptown carries more of special merchandise than apparel. The apparel it carries is also very specific to the KPOP groups

COKODIVE

Cokodive, similar to Kpoptown carries products which are more specific merchandise for the KPOP groups. The options also seem to be limited for groups apart from BTS. 

WHICH STORE IS BEST FOR YOU?

In conclusion, the takeaway from the information on this site is that depending upon your needs as a KPOP fan you might find a specific site more useful than the others. If you are an ARMY (BTS fan) then there is probably no need to choose a particular site as there are ample products on all 3 websites. However, if you are a fan of any of the other three groups you may want to only consider purchasing official merchandise like lightsticks on a site like Cokodive.
